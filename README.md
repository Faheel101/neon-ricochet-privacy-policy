# Privacy Policy — Neon Ricochet

**Published by 4Ever · Effective 21 July 2026 · Contact: faheelanjum12@gmail.com**

This policy explains what information the mobile game **Neon Ricochet** ("the game", "we") handles, how it is used, and the choices you have. We do not require an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other contact details to play.

## Summary

- Your game progress and settings are stored **on your device**.
- Anonymous gameplay and stability analytics are collected **only if you allow them**.
- The game shows ads through **Google AdMob**; personalized ads are used only if you consent.
- You can change these choices at any time from the in-game **Privacy & Data** screen, and delete your local data there or by uninstalling.

## Information stored on your device

To let you keep playing where you left off, the game saves the following locally on your device (using Android app storage and browser local storage). This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us:

- High score, level/progression, and lifetime statistics
- Settings (sound, music, haptics, visual quality, accessibility, chosen theme)
- Unlocked content, missions, daily streaks, and in-game currency ("Prisms")
- Your privacy choice and tutorial completion state

## Analytics (optional — off unless you allow it)

If you choose "Allow analytics" or "Allow all" on the privacy screen, the game may collect anonymous, aggregated events to help us understand difficulty, stability, and where players drop off. These events never include your name or contact information. They may include:

- Gameplay events (e.g. runs started/ended, scores, merges, screens viewed)
- Performance and crash/diagnostic signals
- Technical context such as app version, build channel, and operating-system platform (Android or web)

When transmitted, these events are sent only to a collection endpoint operated by us (4Ever) — never to Google or any advertiser. The current release does not have an analytics endpoint configured, so these events stay on your device and are not sent anywhere. If you choose "Essential only", no analytics are collected at all.

## Advertising

The game displays ads (including rewarded and full-screen ads) served by **Google AdMob**. To show ads, Google may collect and process data such as a device advertising identifier and app-usage signals, in accordance with Google's own policies. You are shown a consent choice on first launch:

- **Essential only** / **Allow analytics** — ads are non-personalized (contextual) where required.
- **Allow all** — ads may be personalized using Google's advertising signals.

Where required by law, a Google-provided consent form is shown before personalized ads are used. You can learn how Google uses this data at [policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites) and review AdMob's practices at [Google's AdMob data disclosure](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543).

## How we use information

- To save and restore your progress and preferences on your device
- To improve balance, difficulty, and stability (only with your analytics consent)
- To display and, with consent, personalize advertising that supports the game

We do not sell your personal information.

## Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Advertising is served by **Google AdMob**, and Google's handling of advertising data is governed by the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). Analytics, when a collection endpoint is enabled, is sent only to servers operated by us (4Ever) — it is not shared with Google or any other third party. The current release does not have an analytics endpoint configured, so analytics data stays on your device.

## Children

Neon Ricochet is a general-audience game and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us and we will address it.

## Your choices and rights

- **Change consent:** open *Settings → Privacy & Data* in the game at any time.
- **Delete local data:** use "Delete local data" on the Privacy & Data screen, or uninstall the app.
- **Reset your advertising ID:** in your device's Android settings under Privacy / Ads.
- Depending on your region (e.g. GDPR, CCPA), you may have rights to access or delete data held about you. Contact us to make a request.

## Data retention and security

On-device data persists until you delete it or uninstall the app. Advertising data is retained by Google according to their policies. Any analytics events transmitted to our endpoint are retained by us (4Ever) in anonymous, aggregated form only. We take reasonable measures to protect the limited data the game handles, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the game evolves or as regulations change. Material changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.
